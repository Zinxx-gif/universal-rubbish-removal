Enniscorthy is north Wexford’s main market town, sitting on the River Slaney and known for its strong community spirit and deep historical roots — including the site of the 1798 Rebellion. We serve Enniscorthy and the surrounding north Wexford area regularly, making the journey from our Kilkenny base to collect from homes and businesses across the region.

The town has a mix of traditional stone houses on the hillside, river-level properties by the Slaney and modern estates on the outskirts. We collect from all of them.
