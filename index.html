Universal Rubbish Removal IE is a professional waste removal service operating across Kilkenny and the South East of Ireland, helping homes and businesses clear unwanted items quickly and affordably.

From a single bulky item to a full truck clearout, we handle everything from start to finish. You only pay for the space you use, with no hidden fees and no skips blocking your driveway.
